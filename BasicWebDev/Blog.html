Why I like this place

Rittenhouse Square is one of my favorite places in Philadelphia. It is in the middle of the city, but it feels quiet and relaxing. I like coming here when I want a break from busy streets and noise.

What you can do here

People come to Rittenhouse Square to walk, sit on benches, or relax on the grass. Sometimes there are musicians playing, and other times people bring their dogs. It is a good place to slow down and enjoy the city.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit is in the afternoon or early evening. In the spring and fall, the weather is nice and the park looks really good. During summer, it can be crowded, but it still feels welcoming.

Things I notice

  • Lots of trees and flowers
  • People reading or talking
  • Dogs and street performers
  • Historic buildings nearby

Quick Facts

Location: Center City, Philadelphia

Best season: Spring and Fall

Good place for: Relaxing, walking, conversation
