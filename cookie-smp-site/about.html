About Cookie SMP

Cookie SMP is a Vanilla Survival Multiplayer server.

Our community values

  • Be respectful (no harassment or hate).
  • Play fair (no cheats, no exploits).
  • Help others (new players are welcome).
  • Keep it friendly (safe chat and good vibes).

Rules (nested list)

These rules keep the server fun for everyone.

  • Chat rules
    • No hate speech, slurs, or targeted bullying.
    • No spam or advertising without permission.
    • Keep public chat PG and friendly.
  • Gameplay rules
    • No hacks, x-ray, dupes, or unfair mods.
    • No griefing or stealing from other players.
    • PvP only when both players agree.
  • Community rules
    • Listen to staff and use reports for problems.
    • Respect claims and community build areas.
    • Be kind to new players.
